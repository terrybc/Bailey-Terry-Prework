About Me

My name is Bailey Christopher Terry. Now I know what you're thinking, "You can't trust someone with three first names", but I happen to be an exception to that rule. My name comes from my strong Irish and British heritage as well as my mother's wish to have a creative first name for her children. When I was a child I used to hate my first name (I wanted my name to be Jake), couldn't spell my middle name, and thought my last name was boring. Now that I am older I love my name as a whole and I have become a lot more interested in my family heritage.

I consider myself to be a very nerdy person. Firstly I am a self-proclaimed shark expert and will be retiring by the ocean so I can study and admire sharks in my late life. Not only am I persuing software engineering as a career but I absolutely love Star Wars - I know way too much lore about the cinematic universe - and I grew up learning all the spells in Harry Potters world (it's LeviOOsa not Leviosaaa). I also am a big video gamer; I love RPG games, survival games, puzzle games, and adventure games. My top three favorite video games are:

  1. Subnautica
  2. Overwatch
  3. Skyrim

I like to stay active in my free time when I'm not gaming. I have my dog, Finn, who I take on walks; I like to go to parks and hike around with my girlfiend; I love to go swimming whenever I have the opportunity; lastly, I am the first to volunteer whenever friends or family members need help around the yard or moving things around the house just to get some sun or movement.
